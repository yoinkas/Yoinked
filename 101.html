Objective

Operation: Starter Terminal

A training server was left behind by a sloppy operator. Your job is to move through the directories, read the right files, and recover three training flags without guessing.

Use the terminal like a real beginner recon workflow: confirm where you are, list what exists, follow the clue trail, and cat the files that matter.

Mission objectives

  1. Use pwd to see where you are.
  2. Use ls to see what is available.
  3. Use whoami to identify the current user.
  4. Move into the missions directory.
  5. Read briefing.txt.
  6. List the files in missions with ls.
  7. Open first-flag.txt and recover the first flag.
  8. Use cd .. to move back one directory.
  9. Search for incoming.flag with find.
  10. Move into Downloads.
  11. Open incoming.flag and recover the second flag.
  12. Use ls -la to reveal hidden files.
  13. Read .secret and recover the final flag.
  14. Move into vault.
  15. Read flag-report.txt to close the mission.
